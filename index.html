Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

ArabiSabak - Learn Gulf Arabic ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

ArabiSabak is designed with privacy in mind. The App operates primarily offline and collects minimal data:

  • Learning Progress: Your lesson progress, bookmarks, and favorite words are stored locally on your device only.
  • App Preferences: Settings such as theme preferences are stored locally on your device.

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

2. Text-to-Speech

The App uses your device's built-in text-to-speech (TTS) engine to pronounce Arabic words and phrases. Audio is processed locally on your device. No voice data is sent to external servers.

3. Data Storage

All user data (progress, favorites, settings) is stored locally on your device using shared preferences. We do not transmit, store, or process your data on any external server.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: The App may download fonts from Google Fonts to display Arabic text. Google's privacy policy applies to this service.
  • Google Play Store: The App is distributed through Google Play, which is governed by Google's privacy policy.

5. Children's Privacy

ArabiSabak is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the App does not collect any personal data, it is safe for use by children.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security settings. We recommend using a screen lock and keeping your device software up to date.

7. Your Rights

You can delete all App data at any time by:

  • Clearing the App's data through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the App

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
